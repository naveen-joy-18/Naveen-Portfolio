About Me

I'm Naveen Joy, a passionate Information Science Engineer. Hailing from Kadaba, Karnataka, I have a deep interest in Machine Learning, Generative AI development and Large Language Models with a focus on API Integration and Data Analysis project designing.

My journey into Python and Generative AI began with a strong passion for Deep Learning models, leading me to present a review paper on DEEP LEARNING and I received a professional Python Developer certification from HackerRank. The interest in Generative AI was built on the focus that the future is a paradise for Prompt Engineers and Generative AI Experimenters.

Alongside Python, I am also proficient in Generative AI, Web Development and DevOps. My Recent Major Project, Automatic Animal and Bird Intrusion Detection System is the best example of the integration of AI, IOT and other modern technologies.

With a unique combination of expertise in Python development, web applications, generative AI, and DevOps, I am committed to leveraging my skills to drive innovative solutions and enhance digital security and functionality.
